1. Your Right to Data Deletion

At APJAKJB Test Portal, we respect your privacy and give you full control over your personal data. In compliance with Google Play's Data Safety policies and global privacy standards, you can request the permanent deletion of your account and all associated data at any time.

2. What Data Will Be Deleted?

When you submit a data deletion request, we will permanently wipe the following information from our Firebase Database and Google Cloud servers:

  • Profile Data: Your registered Name, Email Address, and Device FCM Tokens.
  • Performance Data: All your past test scores, detailed question analytics, and leaderboard rankings.
  • Purchase History: Records of your premium test bundle unlocks.

⚠️ Warning: Account deletion is irreversible. Once your data is deleted, you will lose access to all your purchased premium test series and historical analytics. You cannot recover this data later.

3. How to Request Deletion

To ensure security and verify your identity, we process deletion requests via email. Please follow these steps:

Submit Your Request

Send an email from your registered email address to our support team with the subject line "Account Deletion Request".

Include your full name and a brief confirmation that you wish to permanently delete your data.

📧 Request Data Deletion

4. Processing Time

Once we receive your email, our technical team will verify your identity. Your account and all associated data will be completely wiped from our live servers and backups within 48 hours.
